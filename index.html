App Description

Purpose of App

The purpose of the app is to create an easy to use attendance tracker. Users will be able to add people to a roster, and from there, be able to record attendence.
The innovation comes from the fact that each person in a roster will be assigned a unique qr code in which the application will generate.
From there the app will generate pictures, which will be printed out on to paper. To take attendance, the student can show the instructor the qr code, and he/she will be scanned in.
But there is more. The instructor will be able to ask the whole class to hold up there qr codes.
From there, the instructor can use the camera feature and sweep the room, recording everyones attendance. The application uses the actual clock. So instructors can set time thresholds and absenses.

Target User Group
Teachers at the elementary, middle school and high school level.
Extracurricular activity instructors.
And anyone who needs a quick attendance tracker!
How can this app solve problems?
Many teachers have to take two to five minutes taking attendence at the start of class. They have to look around the room, ask students where their peers are...
Its a somewhat time consuming process.
Our app attempts to speed up this process. While at Bronx Science teachers have to submit pink sheets and bubble in forms, this app can speed things up completely.
The teacher just has to ask students to hold up their qr codes. This will take one to two minutes maximum. And the reports needed are automatically generated
so the teacher can take care of submitting the forms at the end of the day.
With the extra time, teachers can get back to doing what really matters, teaching!
